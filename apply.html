Habitat for Humanity Powhatan (HFHP) is a Christian housing ministry that partners with lower income families to build homes that they can purchase. The homes are sold at the cost of construction and mortgaged with zero-interest loans. Habitat is funded by private donations and utilizes volunteer labor. The three primary criteria for selection are housing need, ability to pay, and the potential for being a good Habitat partner.

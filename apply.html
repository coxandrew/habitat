The three primary criteria for selection are housing need, ability to pay, and the potential for being a good Habitat partner. To qualify for a Habitat home, you must fill out the Homeowner Application and mail it back to us along with copies of recent financial records:

Download Homeowner Application

We know this application can be a little intimidating, so don't hesitate to contact us if you need help or have any questions.
